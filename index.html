About:

SBMF Solutions

SBMF Solutions is a Minority Owned Small Business located in Lilburn, GA that delivers information technology services to the federal government, state and local municipalities. As a full-services IT & consulting company, SBMF Solutions helps customers meet their technology objectives by providing technical resources to meet their short and long term IT requirements. We bring to bare fast, reliable, immediately productive, and experienced resources from multiple disciplines to reduce risk and ensure customer satisfaction. Utilizing customer focused demand management processes, we continuously prepare and develop talent tailored towards your specific needs.
